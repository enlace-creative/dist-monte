Monte de Oración offers lodging for groups of up to 35 people — retreats, camps, or spiritual visits — and for individuals seeking rest in nature, subject to availability.

Lodging

10 private cabins
Kitchen, dining area, living room, full bathroom, and master bedroom. Private terrace with valley views.
Casa Vainilla
Near the main entrance and common areas. 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, spacious kitchen, dining room, living room, and laundry.
Casa del Lago
A quiet, secluded area. 2 bedrooms, fully equipped for a comfortable stay.
Bunkhouse
Poolside, ideal for groups. 4 bunk beds and 2 full bathrooms.

Common areas

Las Brisas
Air-conditioned administrative building with lobby, industrial kitchen, and capacity for 50 people for meetings or meals.
Hall and meeting spaces
Open area for up to 250 guests and an indoor space for 50.
Semi-Olympic pool
Ideal for leisure and recreation with family or groups.
Nature trails
Hiking, exploration, and quiet reflection through the forest.
Prayer chapel
For 50 people: personal communion, weddings, and bridal photos.

The natural surroundings and Central Valley views make every visit a memorable experience.
