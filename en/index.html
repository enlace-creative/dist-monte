Santa Eulalia, Atenas · Costa Rica

A place to encounter God

El Monte (short for Monte de Oración) is a Christian wellness center located in Atenas, at the western end of Costa Rica's Central Valley. It is an ideal place for missionary visits, spiritual retreats, and personal encounters with God.

How to get here

We are located at Santa Eulalia, Atenas, Alajuela, Costa Rica. Santa Eulalia de Atenas is west of the Central Valley, with easy access from San José and the coast.

3.0 Master plan →

Master Plan

The Master Plan, designed by architect Sergio Frugone of VOID, represents a statement of faith and vision for the future of Monte de Oración. This project seeks to become a spiritual oasis that will positively impact generations to come.

Architecture by
Commercial & retailArts & cultureResidential & hospitalitySpiritual areas

Through your stay, you can help provide the funding needed to bring this ambitious vision to life.

Strategically located closest to the main entrance.

The core concept of this architectural vision is to attract visitors— including those who do not identify as Christians— through its commercial and wellness offerings. In this way, the project seeks to naturally and gradually introduce them to the message of the Gospel and God's plan for their lives.
