1.0 What El Monte offers →

What El Monte offers

Three key spaces for your wedding: ceremony, reception, and lodging in one place.

Up to 50 guests

Ceremony

We offer a chapel with seating for up to 50 guests, as well as a variety of outdoor locations where the ceremony can be held with the chapel as a beautiful natural backdrop.

Up to 250 seated guests

Reception

The reception can take place in our main event tent, which comfortably accommodates up to 250 seated guests. We provide support and guidance to ensure that every detail makes this day a meaningful and unforgettable experience.

Up to 35 guests

Lodging

The property also includes a private house where the bride can get ready on the wedding day and stay overnight. With lodging available for up to 35 guests, El Monte is also an excellent choice for destination weddings.
