Visit rules

El Monte is a Christian wellness center. We welcome visitors for retreats, spiritual camps, and family events. Please respect these guidelines when using our facilities.

1.0 Guidelines →

Guidelines

  • Daytime visiting hours are from 5:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

  • Alcohol and any illicit substances are prohibited.

  • Music and obscene language are prohibited.

  • All minors must be accompanied by an adult.

  • Romantic displays of affection are prohibited.

  • Everyone must help maintain order and cleanliness by picking up their trash and, if bringing pets, their waste as well.

  • Please respect spiritual, recreational, or family activities taking place in designated areas such as the event tent or others.

  • Day visitors have access to green areas and marked trails only; entry to event areas or guest-only spaces (such as the pool) is prohibited.
